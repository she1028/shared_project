[FAQ]
Q: How do I book a catering service?
A: You can call us at +63 917 123 4567 or send an email to contact@ymzm-catering.com. We will guide you through the booking process.

Q: How much is the delivery fee?
A: The delivery fee depends on the distance from our kitchen to the event venue. For Metro Manila, delivery is free for orders above ₱30,000. For other areas, the fee will be provided upon inquiry.

Q: Can you accommodate special dietary requests?
A: Yes, we can customize our menu to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary preferences.
